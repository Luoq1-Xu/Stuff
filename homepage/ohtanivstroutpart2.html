Pitch 4 : Another 100MPH Fastball middle-middle.

First, let me show you what Trout can do with a fastball down the middle.

Now, ready for what happens next?

Again, Trout swings and misses on a fastball straight down the middle. Why is that?

  • Firstly, this is still a 100mph Fastball. Even if it sits nicely middle-middle, It is still very difficult to square up.
  • Secondly, notice that Trout was a little late to swing. This means that he may have been guessing another pitch type and was not expecting the Fastball. When the ball is coming in at 100MPH, indecision can be fatal.

As the commentators said : Pure challenge, My best stuff against your best swing.
